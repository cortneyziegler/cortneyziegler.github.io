A little about me...

Hey there, my name is Cortney and I like to look at/create/admire/build maps. I also like things other than maps- things like hiking, reading, camping, baking, hyper fixating on puzzles, and long walks with my dogs. I was born and raised just outside of Los Angeles, and moved to the PNW after completing my undergraduate studies. Of the many things I am that cannot fit into a paragraph, I am nothing if not curious and absolutely willing to dip my toes into unfamiliar territory (of which includes building a web portfolio, but that’s not the point). Maps are my favorite because they feed my curious spirit. They provide visual intersections of information about humans, the environment, geography, and culture, which is pretty rad. See my resume for more information about my education and work experience, and check out my portfolio on the Home page for links to some things I’ve been working on.
